What We Believe

Centered on the gospel and guided by Scripture

We are part of the Christian Church (Disciples of Christ), holding to historic, orthodox Christian belief while inviting questions and dialogue. We believe Jesus calls us to unity, service, and the table of grace for all.

Ask a Pastor

We confess one God in three persons revealed in Scripture. Jesus Christ is the Son of God whose life, death, and resurrection bring salvation and restoration to all creation.

The Bible is our primary witness to God's work and guides our faith and practice. We celebrate weekly communion and welcome all to the Lord's Table.

We follow Jesus together through spiritual practices, service, and justice. We equip every disciple to live out the gospel in the places they live, work, and play.

Kids & Families

Our Kids Ministry partners with families to disciple children from birth through 5th grade. Each Sunday features engaging lessons, worship, and age-appropriate activities. Midweek family nights help parents cultivate faith at home.

  • Secure check-in system and trained volunteers
  • Family resource hub with weekly conversation guides
  • Quarterly family experiences and parenting workshops
Student Ministry

Students experience belonging and purpose through weekly gatherings, student-led worship, and missional serving. Leadership cohorts equip teens to live sent in their schools and neighborhoods.

  • Sunday morning Bible study at 9:15am
  • Wednesday night worship, teaching, and small groups
  • Summer camp and winter retreat each year
Community Groups

Community groups gather weekly in homes to form friendships, study Scripture, and care for one another. We have groups for young adults, families, empty nesters, and more.

  • Choose a group based on location, life stage, or topic
  • Childcare stipends available for groups that need them
  • Quarterly rallies to worship and celebrate stories together
